About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at ISAE-SUPAERO. I am working on the certification of the LustreC compiler (under the supervision of Christophe Garion and Xavier Thirioux).

I am interested in formal methods and their applications to Discrete Event Systems. I am particularly interested in model checking of time models (time Petri nets, timed automata) and parametric time models.

During my PhD, I developed verification methods to check diagnosis properties on systems modeled as Time Petri nets and Parametric Time Petri nets. These methods prevent from synchronization problem of Time Petri nets.
